# Ratu Sovereign AI - Marketing Knowledge Base

## What is Ratu?

Ratu Sovereign AI is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that provides sovereign AI nodes for organizations. Each organization gets its own private AI node with complete data control and zero vendor lock-in.

## Key Features

### 1. True Sovereignty
- Your data stays yours
- Deploy on-premise or air-gapped
- No vendor lock-in
- Complete control over your AI

### 2. Model-Off Training
- Base Kimi K2 model is NEVER retrained
- Knowledge grows only through embeddings (RAG)
- Predictable behavior
- Cryptographically verified daily
- Fully auditable

### 3. Multi-Agent Council
- Multiple AI agents deliberate on complex queries
- Roles: Researcher, Analyst, Editor, Critic
- Three strategies: Deliberate, Consensus, Critic
- Comprehensive, well-reasoned answers

### 4. RAG with Citations
- Every answer cites its sources
- Format: [CIT:doc_id:chunk_ix]
- Track provenance
- Verify accuracy
- Build trust

### 5. Auto-Discovery
- Crawl websites automatically
- Parse PDFs and documents
- Detect changes
- Keep knowledge fresh
- Respect robots.txt

### 6. Voice Capabilities
- Speech-to-text (Whisper, Deepgram)
- Text-to-speech (OpenAI, ElevenLabs)
- Multiple voice options
- Natural conversations

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try Ratu for free?
Yes! We offer a free plan with 1 node and 100 queries/month. No credit card required.

### How is my data protected?
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored in isolated namespaces, and never shared with other organizations. You can export or delete it anytime.

### Can I deploy on-premise?
Yes, Enterprise plans include on-premise deployment options, including air-gapped environments.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?
You can export all your data before canceling. We retain it for 30 days after cancellation, then permanently delete it.

### Do you train models on my data?
No, never. The base Kimi K2 model is frozen. Your knowledge grows only through embeddings, which are private to your organization.

### Can I customize the AI?
Yes! You can configure agents, add custom tools, set system prompts, and more through the dashboard.

### What languages are supported?
Kimi K2 supports multiple languages. The platform UI is currently in English with multi-language support coming soon.

### How do I get help?
Free plans get community support, paid plans get email support, and Enterprise gets 24/7 dedicated support.
